What is the MetaForge Engine?
MetaForge is an autonomous document infrastructure designed for elite enterprises to generate, manage, and verify professional documents with cryptographic security.
Are the documents legally binding?
Our templates are engineered to meet global standards, but we recommend consulting with your legal department for specific jurisdiction requirements.
How does QR verification work?
Each document generated by the engine includes a unique verification hash encoded in a QR code. Scanning this link directs users to our secure validation portal to confirm authenticity.
Can I use custom branding?
Yes, the MetaForge Creator allows full control over typography, color tokens, and brand assets to ensure perfect alignment with your corporate identity.
