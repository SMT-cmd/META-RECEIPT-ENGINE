MASTERING THE ENGINE

Welcome! This Branded Engine is now yours. Here is how to use the Control Panel effectively:

⚙️ The Control Panel

Tap the Gear Icon at the top right to access all hidden tools.

  • Document Type: Switch between Invoice, Receipt, or Estimate. The system automatically updates the status stamp.
  • Toggles: Control the visibility of VAT, Digital Stamp, Security Watermark, and the Verification QR Code.
  • Stamp Customization: Change the authority name (e.g., "Manager") and status (e.g., "VERIFIED") directly from the panel.
  • Authorized Signature: Upload your signature once; it saves locally and appears on every document you generate.
  • History Manager: Tap the 📜 icon in the panel to view, edit, or export your entire generation history.
💡 Pro Tip: You can edit any text on the receipt by clicking directly on it! No need to use the panel for names or items.
📲 App Mode: Use the "Install as App" button in the panel to add this engine to your home screen. It works perfectly offline!
